Tokyo 2020

Fifty-six years after having organized the Olympic Games,
the capital of Japan will be hosting a Summer edition for
the second time, from 24 July to 9 August 2020.

Date

July 24th-August 9th

Country

Japan

Countdown

136 Days

Sports

Gymnastics

Artistic gymnastics has been
part of the Olympic programme
since the first modern Games of
Athens 1896. Initially for men
only, women's events were
included from Amsterdam 1928.
